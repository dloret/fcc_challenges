What is trail running?

According to Wikipedia trail running is a sport combining running and hinking in wilderness. It is done generally done over longer distances and involve significant amounts of ascent. This is the main difference with cross country running, which is done over shorter distances. This is a recently fashionable sport. Most of the famous races over the globe were founded in the beginning of the 21st century. One of the oldest and most famous trail races in Europe is the UTMB, "Ultra Trail du Mont Blanc".
